關於本作品｜About US

以小丑為主角是提倡人人平平等的概念，不論誰都可以成爲英雄。
另外，我們在游戲故事中也提到了關於馬戲團動物表演背後的辛酸，希望玩家在體驗後除了可以接受平凡的自己，也以不同角度去看挖掘生活或是這個世界中仍然存在的問題。

With mixed reality and haptic technologies, "WaterForm" utilizes liquid transformation to generate hybrid feedback to enhance the immersive environment.
The demonstration features a multisensory relaxation experience set in a virtual Eastern landscape, allowing for immersive narratives to explore self-awareness.
