關於本作品｜About US

躺平（Goblin Mode）是2021年開始流行的網路詞語以及2022牛津年度代表字。指90後和00後年輕人在國內經濟下滑、社會階層固定導致的階層流動困難及疫情造成的社會問題激化的大背景下出於對現實環境的失望而做出的「與其跟隨社會期望堅持奮鬥，不如選擇『躺平』，無欲無求」的處事態度。
而哥布林（Goblin）幾乎在所有中世紀背景的遊戲世界中，最低等的新手怪物，通常被描繪成性格卑鄙卻也不太聰明的一種黑暗種族。
「毫無帶歉意地放縱、懶惰、享樂的行為，並表現出一種拒絕社會規範或期待的態度。」
哥布林保育協會希望透過IP的形式能讓大眾深入了解躺平，並擺脫對躺平的刻版印象。

Goblin Mode is an internet term that became popular in 2021 and was selected as the Oxford Word of the Year for 2022. It refers to a coping attitude adopted by young people born in the 1990s and 2000s in response to economic decline, social immobility, and exacerbated societal issues due to the COVID-19 pandemic. It reflects disillusionment with the prevailing societal expectations and the difficulty of social mobility, leading individuals to choose a mindset of goblin mode and live a life of contentment and minimal desires, rather than striving to meet societal expectations.
In medieval-themed gaming worlds, goblins are typically portrayed as the lowest-tier beginner monsters, characterized by their despicable nature and lack of intelligence, representing a dark and primitive race.
"It embodies indulgence, laziness, and hedonism without remorse, demonstrating a refusal to adhere to social norms or expectations."
The Goblin Conservation Society aims to promote a deeper understanding of goblin mode through various intellectual properties (IPs), seeking to dispel misconceptions and associated stereotypes.
