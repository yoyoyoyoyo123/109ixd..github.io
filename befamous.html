Befamous!
『US』 作品一覽 關於我們

關於本作品｜About US

透過一個名為BeFamous! 的虛擬平台，揭示了AI技術如何被用來迅速提升個人在社交媒體上的可見度與流量，同時指出這種迅速獲得關注的過程可能伴隨著隱私的大量洩露和個人資訊的不自覺共享與篡改。
挑戰觀眾重新思考「紅」的意義，以及AI所帶來的不僅是正面的成就，也可能使人們暴露於未知風險中，喪失對於自身形象控制權的潛在危險。

BeFamous!, this work profoundly reveals the astonishing capability of AI technology in enhancing individual visibility on social media, while simultaneously warning the invasion of privacy and unconscious sharing and alteration of personal information.
We challenge the audience to rethink the meaning of 'fame' and to consider that the impact of AI is not only positive achievements but also the potential danger of exposing individuals to unknown risks and losing control over their own images.
