關於本作品｜About US

《US》是一個虛擬實境的互動式畢業展，同時也是一個沉浸式劇情體驗。
我們結合了畢籌單位以及系上的所有組別的作品，打造出一個完整且與眾不同的線上展場。

US is an interactive graduation exhibition in virtual reality, and it is also an immersive plot experience.
We combined the works of all the departments to create a complete and unique online exhibition. Through continuous exploration, the audience will discover different ideas and gradually understand what gods are and what is the key of interaction design.
