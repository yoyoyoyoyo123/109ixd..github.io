109 級互動設計系｜109th Department of Interaction Design

國立臺北科技大學互動設計系，成立目標是為配合我國政府的文化政策，旨在強調「提升本土藝術，結合最新科技，融入國際市場」
作品形式與技術包含擴增實境 (AR)、虛擬實境 (VR)、混和實境 (MR)、大型互動裝置、情境互動投影、多媒體動畫、遊戲與APP應用設計
主要特色在於橫跨電子科技、人文、設計與藝術等多項領域，以「互動設計、互動藝術、互動娛樂」作為三大核心架構
期望透過跨領域整合設計的成果帶給人們生活更創新多元的想像與應用

The Department of Interactive Design at National Taipei University of Technology was established to comply with the cultural policy of the Chinese government and aims to emphasize“ Enhancing local art, integrating the latest technology, and integrating into the international market.”
Work forms and technologies include Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR), large-scale interactive installations, situational interactive projections, multimedia animations, games and APP application design.
The main feature is that it spans multiple fields such as electronic technology, humanities, design and art, with "interactive design, interactive art, and interactive entertainment" as the three core structures We hope that the results of cross-disciplinary integrated design will bring more innovative and diverse imaginations and applications to people's lives.
