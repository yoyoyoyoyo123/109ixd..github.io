關於本作品｜About US

我們從自身經驗出發，發現旁觀者在霸凌事件中有很重要的影響力。
因此，拜訪網站的體驗者將化身為霸凌事件的旁觀者，透過一連串的選擇主導霸凌事件的走向。我們希望傳達的是：若旁觀者願意站出來，是能夠阻止霸凌的。

Based on our experiences, we found that bystanders play a crucial role in bullying.
Therefore, we make visitors of our website the bystander in bullying. They can affect the plot by making different decisions. We want everyone to know that it is possible to prevent bullying if bystanders are willing to step up to the plate and take positive actions.
