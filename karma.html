Karma
『US』 作品一覽 關於我們

關於本作品｜About US

世界的盡頭是什麼?盡頭之後，萬物回歸初始，循環往復，生生不息，也象徵著生命的循環。
選擇種下了因，產生業力推動故事前進，走向最終的盡頭。遠古時期從魚演化成人類，人的選擇推動故事走向哪種結局呢?

What is the end of the world? After the end, all things revert to their original state, perpetuating an endless cycle that symbolizes the essence of life.
Choices serve as the catalyst for sowing the seeds of cause, enabling the generation of karma that propels one's narrative toward its ultimate ending. Upon reflection, considering the evolution from fish to humans, what result of human choices will lead to the ultimate ending of the world?
