沒有人類的世界，會不會更美好？
每個「人造」進步的背後，往往都是以開發自然為代價。環保的普及雖然看似減緩了人類對大自然的傷害，我們卻難以否認人類活動仍持續對地球造成著破壞。多年後地球上留下的，是否只有對土地深深的「眷戀」？
《人造眷戀》希望透過引人深思的遊戲機制和故事情節，作為啟發思考的媒介，呼籲玩家重新思考人類與自然之間的共存方式。

Would Earth be a better place without humanity's existence?
“Core”is the essence of all life, providing energy. In "The Verge of Desire", players explore the delicate balance between Core Energy utility and environmental preservation. Players navigate challenges with Core Energy, which is vital for exploration but potentially devastating to the environment when overused.
Through the mechanics and storyline, the game prompts reflection on the intricate relationship between humanity and Mother Nature.
