黑暗喜劇是一款主打全黑的VR遊戲，玩家將扮演一位小男孩在奇幻的空間內探索，在神秘角色的引領下，尋找前往「塔」的道路。在過程中玩家可以使用四種不同的玩具來發出聲波，利用聲波掃描周圍的環境，幫助玩家在全黑的視野中探索前進；每一種玩具發出的聲波樣式各有不同，並且都有不同的功用。
前往塔的過程危機四伏，在使用聲波探索時請小心來自黑暗中的危險!

A pitch black VR game where you literally explore the "dark" mind of a visitor from space. To help you see in the dark, we have hidden 4 sound-making tools with different play styles throughout the map, making every run just a little bit different. The world you will see in the game is not at all like our own, as it was built by the visitor with views from its long journey through the cosmos. You will also not be alone in the dark, sound-sensitive aliens lurk in every corner, ready to stop you in your tracks.
Take heart as you journey to the depths of the abyss and uncover A DARK JOKE FROM DARK SPACE.
