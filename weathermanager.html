關於本作品｜About US

天氣的差別沒有所謂的好跟壞，只有人本身怎麼去看待而已，就像有人喜歡雨天、有人喜歡天氣陰陰的、有人害怕閃電等等。這也像是人們在面對事情時所呈現的樣貌會各有差異，在本質上沒有對錯，卻會造成彼此之間的誤解跟摩擦。
我們希望可以藉由天氣凸顯不同面相的差異會如何造成關係上的誤解跟紛爭。

The difference in weather isn't inherently good or bad; it's all about how individuals perceive it. Just like some people enjoy rainy days, others prefer overcast weather, and some may fear lightning, etc. This is akin to the varied reactions people have when facing situations; there's no inherent right or wrong, yet it can lead to misunderstandings and friction between individuals.
We hope to illustrate how highlighting the differences in various aspects of weather can lead to misunderstandings and conflicts in relationships.
