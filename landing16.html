『US』 作品一覽 關於我們

US: The Key of Interaction Design

過去我們總認為推著我們前進的，是偉大且崇高的神，然而當我們透過觀察、交流、創作、回饋來完整祂、為祂填上色彩。
「 回頭想想，神究竟是誰？」此視覺將本系參與展覽的所有組別名稱放上並加上編號，調查各組別專題創作的主題，將其編碼對應至神像身上，點亮神像。

In the past, we always believed that it was a great and noble god who pushed us forward, however, as we complete him, give him color through observation, communication, creation, and feedback.
“ Looking back, who exactly is God? ”This visual will display the names of all participating groups in the exhibition, along with numbers, investigate the themes of the creative works of each group, encode them onto the image of God, and illuminate the statue.
