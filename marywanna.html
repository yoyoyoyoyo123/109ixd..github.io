關於本作品｜About US

透過文獻的研究，我們了解到大麻會增強感官體驗，使人更加敏感和覺知自我感受，藉此能更進一步探索自己的身體。我們將創造一個開放、自由、安全的空間，讓參與者感受大麻的感官擴大效應，並透過客觀認知，自信了解自己身體的需求，沉浸視聽化的體驗。
我們希望每個人都能對此話題保有開放的態度，帶著好奇的心開始，帶著滿足的感受結束，身心靈都因此沉澱。

Through literature research, we understand that maryjuana enhances sensory experiences, making individuals more sensitive and aware of their own feelings, thereby enabling them to further explore their bodies. We will create an open, free, and safe space for participants to experience the sensory expansion effects of maryjuana and, through objective perception, confidently understand their body's needs, immersing in an audiovisual experience.
We hope that everyone can maintain an open attitude towards this topic, starting with a curious heart, ending with a fulfilled feeling, and thus settling in body, mind, and spirit.
