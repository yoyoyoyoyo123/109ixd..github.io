關於本作品｜About US

在《山海經:窮奇》中，我們的核心創新是將先進的 ChatGPT 技術融入遊戲的AI NPC設計。這些NPC不再是單一任務的發布者，而是擁有豐富情感和深度故事的智能角色。
我們的目標是透過這些AI NPC提供一種全新的遊戲互動體驗，讓玩家能夠真正地與遊戲世界中的角色進行互動和溝通。這種設計不僅增強了遊戲的沉浸感和敘事深度，也為玩家帶來了更個性化和獨特的遊戲體驗。通过這種創新，我們期待為玩家創造一個活生生、充滿互動的遊戲世界。

In "Qiongqi," our core innovation lies in integrating advanced ChatGPT technology into the design of AI NPCs. These NPCs are no longer mere task dispensers, but intelligent characters with rich emotions and deep stories.
Our aim is to offer a novel interactive experience in gaming, enabling players to genuinely interact and communicate with the characters in the game world. This design not only enhances the immersion and narrative depth of the game but also provides players with a more personalized and unique gaming experience. With this innovation, we look forward to creating a lively, interactive world for our players.
