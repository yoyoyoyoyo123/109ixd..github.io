關於本作品｜About US

真情實感投入的戀愛，在一瞬間被否決切割。瞬間帶來的負能量，常常讓人感到窒息。
為了幫助被分手者，重獲新生。訂製了七七四十九天的超渡儀式，搭配AI專員陪伴走出傷痛。

The deeply invested love, filled with genuine emotions, is abruptly severed in an instant. The negative energy that comes instantaneously often makes one feel suffocated.
In order to assist those who have been through a breakup in finding a fresh start, a customized seventy-seven-day ritual is designed, accompanied by an AI specialist to help them walk out of their pain.
